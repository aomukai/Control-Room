# Character: Mara Chen

## Basic Info
- **Age:** 28
- **Occupation:** Private Investigator
- **Location:** Neo-Seattle

## Appearance
- Dark hair, often tied back
- Sharp, observant eyes
- Usually wears practical clothes
- Has a small scar above her left eyebrow

## Personality
- Determined and resourceful
- Struggles with trust issues
- Dry sense of humor
- Fiercely loyal to few close friends

## Background
Former police detective who left the force after uncovering corruption.
Now works independently, taking cases others won't touch.

## Motivations
- Seeking justice for the voiceless
- Finding the truth about her father's disappearance
