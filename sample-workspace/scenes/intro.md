# Scene: Introduction

## Setting
A dimly lit coffee shop on a rainy evening. The smell of fresh espresso fills the air.

## Characters Present
- Mara (protagonist)
- The Stranger

## Description
The rain tapped against the window in a steady rhythm. Mara sat alone at her usual table,
nursing a lukewarm latte and staring at the empty seat across from her.

"Is this seat taken?"

She looked up to find a stranger with kind eyes and a worn leather jacket.

## Notes
- Establish mysterious atmosphere
- Hint at Mara's loneliness
- Introduce the Stranger's enigmatic nature
